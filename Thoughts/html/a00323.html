enum Thoughts.Game.Attributes.Attribute.NeedPriority
strong

The levels of priority an attribute can have.

Enumerator
SelfActualization 

Level of priority = 5. MINIMUM level of priority

Examples: Becoming the most that one can be, creative activities, ...

Esteem 

Level of priority = 4.

Examples: Respect,Self-esteem, status, recognition/prestige, strength, freedom, accomplishment, ...

Love 

Level of priority = 3.

Examples: Friendship, intimacy, family, sense of connection, ...

Safety 

Level of priority = 2.

Examples: Personal security, employment, resources, heath, property, ...

Physiological 

Level of priority = 1. MAXIMUM level of priority

Examples: Hydration, satiety, shelter, sleep/Rest, clothing, reproduction, warmth, ...

None 

Level of priority = 0. No priority. If it is not a need, it should have this level of priority

Examples: Objects like water (not hydration), wood, berries (not satiety), ...
